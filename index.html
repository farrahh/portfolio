Intro

A bit about me.

I am a full-stack developer who loves to problem solve and make the world a better place. My concern for the environment and social justice is reflected in my educational trajectory: a B.S. in Environmental Biology and a Master of Social Work (M.S.W.). But, when I started helping my husband’s medical startup with UI/UX, I realized that if I really want to maximize my impact in the world, tech is the way to go.

In order to jumpstart my career in tech, I enrolled in and completed an immersive 14-week on-site full stack web development program at Coding Dojo, located in the heart of Silicon Valley. There I was introduced to the industry’s most relevant and critical technologies, and learned a full stack: Python, MEAN, and Ruby on Rails. I took a job as a web developer for a branding company, hoping to make the world a better place for my clients.
